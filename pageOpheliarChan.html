Opheliar Chan

"Understanding and Developing a Mature Application Security Program"
Wednesday Oct 24 |11:00 AM – 12:00 NOON | Room 5 - Management Track

Building an application security program can be daunting. Where do you start? What are the steps to building a mature program from the ground up? Many organizations never achieve a clear vision of what they want their program to look like, or what they need in a program to meet business needs, and therefore never have a clear plan of attack.

Understanding the approaches and elements of a mature application security program is the key to seeing and dealing with the big picture – more importantly, knowing what you need, what you can get away with not having, and being able to grow the program over time will greatly assist in making the program a success.

This presentation discusses the key elements of a mature application security program: what they are, why you should do them, when you should do them, and what value they offer. It will also cover the menu of security testing, security consulting services, and security training categories most often offered by vendors. The audience will be provided with a no-nonsense explanation of what each of these actually are, and how they fit in to and address the big picture.

Opheliar is a senior security consultant for TELUS Security Solutions with a focus on application and infrastructure security. She has amassed a wealth of experience and insight through the management of security testing and compliance programs for big banks, global insurance companies, and government services. Opheliar has a passion for communicating knowledge, especially as related to security. Her distaste for fluffy marketing/sales lingo means that she is constantly seeking ways to explain her work and industry to others in plain English.
