Threats to the data centre are becoming more surgical just as the definition of the data centre morphs. The question becomes, how do you protect applications in an era of elastic data centres?

Michael Martin is a Systems Engineer at Fortinet working as an Advanced Technologies Specialist. Michael began his security career 14 years ago at Check Point Software Technologies where he spent eight years in a variety of roles including support, escalations, training and professional services. He currently specializes in Web Application Firewall technologies and Database Security solutions for Fortinet and serves as a technical overlay for the entire North, Central, and South America regions. Prior to joining Fortinet in October 2011, Michael was a founding member of the Imperva professional services team launched in 2007.
