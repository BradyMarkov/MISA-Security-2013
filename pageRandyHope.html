Message from Mayor Randy Hope

On behalf of Council, staff and citizens of the Municipality of Chatham-Kent, I welcome all delegates, vendors and speakers participating in the 2013 MISA Security Conference!

The Municipality of Chatham-Kent recognizes the role and importance of information technology as an engine for global economic and social development. Information technology investments are an increasingly important area of focus for elected officials and administrative leadership.

While in Chatham-Kent, I hope you have the opportunity to explore our green, progressive and friendly community, which offers the best of urban and rural life for people of all ages...

Sincerely,

Randy R. Hope
Mayor/CEO
Municipality of Chatham-Kent
